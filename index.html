Snub Mares

Welcome to snubmares.com! Originally a personal project to learn the basics of web development and UI design, I have now decided to publish it to share my thoughts on things that I am interested in. My main area of interest is in Aerospace - more specifically in Computational Fluid Dynamics and Numerical Analysis. However, I plan to share content on a wide variety of topics. The name of the website is inspired by the snub disphenoid, and you can read more about it in the blog section. The entirety of this website is a work in progress, so don't be too pressed if something does not work yet!
